# AI Video Editor (CLI)
A command-line tool designed to optimize and automate video editing workflows using AI.

## Project Vision
To empower creators (especially for YouTube and long-form content) with an intelligent, efficient, and developer-friendly tool that automates the tedious parts of video production, such as trimming and metadata optimization, without the overhead of a graphical interface.

## Target Audience
- **YouTube Creators:** Focusing on long-form content optimization.
- **Developers & Power Users:** Who prefer a CLI-first approach and automation.
- **Anyone:** Seeking a scalable way to process video content using AI logic.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Automation & Efficiency:** Reducing manual editing time by delegating repetitive tasks to AI.
- **Logic-First Design:** Focusing on robust processing engines rather than UI/UX overhead.
- **Config-as-Documentation:** Transparent and versionable editing configurations.

## Key Features (V1)
- **AI Smart Trimming:** Automatically detect and remove silences, filler words, or redundant segments based on AI analysis.
- **CLI Interface:** A powerful and flexible command-line interface for local processing.
- **Logic-Centric Processing:** Modular architecture focused on video analysis and manipulation engines.
